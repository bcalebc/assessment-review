Life of Pi

2012

This is a fun tribute page to one of my favorite movies!

Life of Pi is special to me because it is about the intersection of religion and life experience. I saw the movie for the first time in theaters with my mother!


    Things I love about the movie.
  1. There is a tiger in the movie...I love tigers.
  2. The movie seems to take an open minded view of the personal side of religion.
  3. It is an exciting adventure movie.
